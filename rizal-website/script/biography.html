Early Life

José Rizal was born on June 19, 1861 in Calamba, Laguna. From a young age, he showed exceptional intellect and passion for learning.

Education

He studied in Manila, Spain, France, and Germany—earning degrees in medicine and philosophy while mastering 22 languages.

Exile & Execution

Despite advocating nonviolent reform, Rizal was executed on December 30, 1896, becoming a martyr for Filipino independence.
